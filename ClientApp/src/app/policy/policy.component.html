Collected information

We only collect the information that you voluntarily share with us.
Except for your email address, every piece of information we collect is entirely optional and is not required to use the platform.
No data is collected during your platform utilisation, such as log, cookie or device data.

How we use your information

The information you share with us will only be used by us to keep in contact with you.
The information will never be shared or sold with any third parties.

For how long do we keep your information

The information collected will be kept for as long as your account remains active.
Once your account is deleted so is all your information.
